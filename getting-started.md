# Getting Started with CAT ANALYZER

This guide will help you create an account, understand the platform, and take your first steps with CAT ANALYZER.

## Creating Your Account

### Step 1: Navigate to the Platform

1. Go to the CAT ANALYZER website
2. You'll see the homepage with information about the platform
3. Click **"Sign In"** or **"ANALYZER"** to access the login page

### Step 2: Sign Up

1. On the login page, click the **"Signup"** tab
2. Fill in the required information:
   - **Email**: Your work email address
   - **Password**: Create a secure password (minimum 6 characters)
   - **Confirm Password**: Re-enter your password
   - **First Name**: Your first name
   - **Last Name**: Your last name
   - **Affiliation**: Your organization (e.g., "Metropolitan Planning Organization", "State DOT")
   - **User Type**: Select your role (e.g., "Transportation Analyst", "Air Quality Analyst")
3. Click **"Signup"**

> **Note**: The signup process may take a few moments as the system creates your account. You'll see a progress message during this time.

### Step 3: Log In

1. After successful signup, you'll be automatically switched to the **"Login"** tab
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **"Login"**
4. You'll be taken to the Welcome page

## Understanding Your Subscription

When you first create an account, you'll have a **Free** subscription tier. This includes:

- Limited number of orders per month (typically 5)
- Access to all visualization and analysis tools
- Ability to download your order results
- Access to the CAT Grand Database for viewing public data

To see subscription options and upgrade:
- Navigate to **"Plans & Pricing"** in the sidebar (under INFO section)
- Or visit your **[Account](account-management.md)** page to see your current subscription

## Navigating the Platform

### Main Interface

After logging in, you'll see:

- **Sidebar (Left)**: Navigation menu organized into sections:
  - **MENU**: Main features (Welcome, Visualizer, Calculator, Reporter, View Orders, New Order)
  - **TOOLS**: Additional tools (Data Hub, Aggregator, Translators)
  - **LEARN**: Documentation and system status
  - **INFO**: Plans, terms, and additional information
- **Top Navigation Bar**: Logo, CAT branding, and Account link
- **Main Content Area**: Changes based on what you select from the sidebar

### Welcome Page

When you first log in, you'll see the **Welcome** page with three main sections:

1. **Explore Your Data**
   - Visualizer: Interactive charts and maps
   - Calculator: Scenario analysis
   - Data Hub: Bulk downloads

2. **Customize Your Scenarios**
   - New Order: Create emissions modeling orders
   - View Orders: Manage your existing orders

3. **Share & Report**
   - Share orders with colleagues
   - Reporter: Generate Word document reports
   - Account: Manage verification status

### Key Pages Overview

**View Orders**
- See all your submitted orders in a table
- Check order status (processing, ready, etc.)
- Download results
- View order details

**New Order**
- Submit new emissions modeling requests
- Select county and year
- Optionally upload custom MOVES input files

**Visualizer**
- Interactive charts and maps
- Compare different scenarios
- Explore emissions data visually

**Calculator**
- Adjust activity measures (VMT, vehicles, etc.)
- See how emissions change in real-time
- Compare baseline vs. adjusted scenarios

**Reporter**
- Generate professional Word document reports
- Customize charts and data presentation
- Download ready-to-use reports

## Your First Order

Ready to create your first emissions modeling order? Here's a quick overview:

1. **Navigate to New Order**: Click "New Order" in the sidebar (MENU section)
2. **Select Geographic Area**: 
   - Choose by county name or FIPS code
   - Example: "Tompkins County, NY" or FIPS code "36109"
3. **Select Year**: Choose the year you want to model (e.g., 2025)
4. **Optional: Upload Files**: If you have custom MOVES input files, you can upload them
5. **Submit**: Click "Submit Order"
6. **Wait for Processing**: Orders typically take 5-10 minutes to process
7. **View Results**: Go to "View Orders" to see your order status and download results

For detailed instructions, see **[Creating Orders](creating-orders.md)**.

## Understanding Order Status

When you view your orders, you'll see different status messages:

- **Processing**: Your order is being calculated (usually 5-10 minutes)
- **Ready**: Your order is complete and data is available for download
- **Error**: Something went wrong - check order details or contact support

## Tips for New Users

1. **Start Simple**: Create your first order without custom files to see how the system works
2. **Explore Visualizations**: Once you have an order ready, try the Visualizer to see your data
3. **Check the Calculator**: Use the Calculator to see how emissions change with different activity levels
4. **Generate a Report**: Try the Reporter to create a professional document from your data
5. **Review Your Subscription**: Check your account page to understand your order limits
